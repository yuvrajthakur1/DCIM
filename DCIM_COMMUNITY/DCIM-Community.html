Educators Community

Purpose: The Educators Community aims to bring together teachers and trainers across the world to share knowledge and ideas for imparting Digital Citizenship and Internet Maturity (DCIM) education. This community would also support new aspiring educators to choose DCIM education as their career.

Principals Community

Purpose: The Principals community aims to bring school and educational leaders together to address the growing problem of Misuse, Overuse and Risky use of the Internet among students by introducing DCIM as a subject. The community shall discuss and share the best practices to start the DCIM program in school and run it successfully.

Career Counselors Community

Purpose: Counselors play a critical role in guiding students on their educational and career paths. This community focuses on enhancing counselors' understanding of DCIM to better support students in the 21st-century digital landscape. The community shall discuss & share the best practices of advising students for making the best use of the Internet in choosing higher education avenues and career paths.

Psychologist Community

Purpose: In response to the rising challenges of screen, gaming, and social media addiction, along with issues related to inappropriate content, this community is dedicated to empowering the psychologist to guide students to transition from unproductive online activities to productive ones by developing Digital Citizenship and Internet Maturity (DCIM) skills.

Parents community

Purpose: This community is for Parents of children of age group between 13 to 20. The community aims to empower the parents to address the growing problem of Misuse, Overuse and Risky use of the Internet among children by leading them towards Internet Maturity.

Multi stakeholders Community

Purpose: This community includes Parents, Principals, Educators, Psychologists, career counselors, government officials, Cyber security experts and social workers. The community aims to make the mission #EveryChildInternetMature successful
